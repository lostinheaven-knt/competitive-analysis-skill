# Example: B2B SaaS Analysis

## Scenario
Analyze competitors for a mid-market SaaS product, such as HR software, CRM, analytics, or support tooling.

## Example scope
- category: SaaS workflow tool
- region: one country or global, depending on the ask
- customer: SMB, mid-market, or enterprise
- channel: direct sales plus website
- pricing: subscription-based
- goal: identify direct competitors and substitutes

## Likely method mix
- vendor directories and analyst landscapes
- official feature pages and pricing pages
- customer case overlap
- review site and community signal checks
- shortlist based on workflow overlap, buyer overlap, and pricing or deployment fit

## Likely high-weight metrics
- feature fit
- customer overlap
- pricing model fit
- integration depth
- growth or momentum proxies when credible

## Typical output
- 8-20 name candidate pool
- 5-10 filtered names
- 3-5 direct competitors
- explicit substitute bucket

## Reminder
For SaaS, a product with similar features may still be a weak commercial competitor if the buyer, segment, deployment model, or contract size is very different.
