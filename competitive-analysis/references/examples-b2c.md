# Example: B2C Brand Analysis

## Scenario
Analyze competitors for a mid-priced beverage or snack brand selling through e-commerce and national retail channels.

## Example scope
- category: packaged beverage or snack
- region: national market
- customer: mass consumer
- channel: e-commerce plus retail
- price band: mid-market
- goal: identify 3-5 core competitors

## Likely method mix
- category leaders from reports and retail observation
- e-commerce ranking and pricing checks
- social buzz and review signals
- shortlist based on overlap in category, price, customer, and channel

## Likely high-weight metrics
- sales or sell-through proxy
- channel reach
- price position
- brand attention
- review signals

## Typical output
- 10-15 name candidate pool
- 5-8 filtered names
- 3-5 direct competitors
- 2-3 watchlist or adjacent names

## Reminder
Do not treat every large consumer brand as a direct competitor. Category, price tier, and channel overlap matter.
